It describes his time studying physics at top universities in America and Europe. It explains how he became a famous professor who taught many students.Oppenheimer led the Manhattan Project that built the first atomic bomb. It shows how Oppenheimer used his mind to change the world, for better and worse. His work impacts us still today. This is the fascinating life story of J.
